Newsletter

The Adriatic arrives in your inbox every two weeks with a curated selection of what we've been reading, writing, and thinking about.

Each edition brings together our latest essays and editor's picks, short dispatches from across the region, and conversations with the people shaping Adriatic business, politics, and culture.

It's also how we gather. Over the years we've cultivated a community of readers who share our curiosity about the region and the wider world, and the newsletter is where we announce the events that bring us together: talks, dinners, and our annual Strategic Foresight gathering in Ljubljana.

No daily digests, no algorithmic clutter. Just a fortnightly briefing from our corner of Europe, and an invitation to be part of the conversation.
