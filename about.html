About Us

The Adriatic is the publishing arm of the Institute for Strategic Solutions (ISR), a Ljubljana-based think-tank and consultancy working since 2011 to help businesses connect, position themselves, and navigate complexity.

What began as Strategic Foresight, a bulletin tracking shifts in politics, markets, and regional dynamics, has evolved into something more ambitious. The Adriatic is a publication for readers who want to understand the forces shaping this region and the world beyond it. We publish essays, reportage, and analysis on politics, business, and culture, with a perspective rooted in the Adriatic but never confined by it.

Our motto is simple: follow the current, not the noise. In a media landscape dominated by speed and reaction, we aim for depth, clarity, and work that endures beyond the news cycle.

The Adriatic appears online throughout the year, with an annual print edition marking each year's most essential reading.
