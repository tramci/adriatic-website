Our credo

Follow the current, not the noise.

We began as Strategic Foresight — a publication founded in 2011 to map the intersection of geopolitics, economics, and long-term trends. For nearly a decade, we examined risk, transformation, and the forces shaping the Adriatic region and beyond.

By 2019, the world had changed faster than foresight could predict. What once felt like the future had become the present. The boundaries between business, politics, and culture blurred; analysis demanded context, not just data. We responded by evolving into — a magazine for those navigating this new complexity.

Today, The Adriatic stands at the confluence of policy and perspective. We write for decision-makers, strategists, and the globally minded — readers who understand that economics is never just about numbers, and politics never just about power. Our aim is simple: to interpret change, not merely record it.

We publish online throughout the year and in print annually, combining analysis with narrative journalism. Each issue is an attempt to explain where the region fits in the wider world — and where the wider world, in turn, is heading.
