Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

The Adriatic is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services.

What We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the website:

  • Server logs: Basic technical information (IP address, browser type, pages visited) may be temporarily logged for security and operational purposes.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address to send you updates.
  • Subscriber information: If you become a paid subscriber, we collect the information necessary to process your subscription.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising trackers
  • We do not build profiles of our readers
  • We do not use cookies for tracking

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for specific functions (payment processing, email delivery). These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at the Institute for Strategic Solutions.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted on this page.
